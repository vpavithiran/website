Technical Events

1. Hackathon

➼ 2 or 3 members per team.
➼ 2 rounds will be conducted.
➼ Presentation should involve Modern solution, Technique and technology that you are going to use,Creativity and originality of the content.
➼ Presentation time will be 5 to 8 minutes.
➼ Participants are requested to bring the laptops for their presentation with the internet.

2. Code Machine

➼ 2 members per team.
➼ Round 1: (MCQ Madness Mania)
15 MCQ type questions and 15 minutes will be provided and no negative marks.
➼ Round 2: (Code-Craft Chronicles)
Sample test cases given for each question.
Participations are needed to pass those test cases to enter for the final round.
➼ Round 3: (Algorithmic Apex Challenge)
Provided are example test cases for every questions.
Winners will be determined based on the efficiency of their algorithm and logical reasoning.
➼ Selection is based on the duration of solving questions and the number of test cases successfully addressed.
➼ Questions presented are at a basic to intermediate difficulty level ➼ Mobile phones and smart devices are strictly prohibited.

➼ Required skill: C, C++, Java, Python, coupled with foundational programming, DSA

3.Quiz

➼ 2 members per team.
➼ Round 1: 20 questions and 20 minutes for each team.
➼ Round 2: 7 teams will qualify.
➼ Required skill: Python, Web Technology.
➼ Any irregularity will result in immediate disqualification for your team.
➼ Internet will be prohibited.

4. Code Decode

➼ 2 members per team.
➼ Participants are allowed only from Computer Science background
➼ Three rounds will be conducted.
➼ Disqualification for Smart Device Use.
➼ Questions are set at a level ranging from basic to intermediate.
➼ Required skill: Python, SQL, Basic level Data Science questions.

Non-Technical Events

1.Treasure Hunt

➼ Maximum 3 members per Team.
➼ Any number of teams is alllowed from any departments.
➼ The participants should not use any source from the internet.
➼ This is a time bount event for finding the treasure.
➼ Mobile Phones are allowed with good speed of internet.
➼ If the participants or team use the open sources to find the answers on during the event that team will be disqualified immediately.
➼ Participants should use only one phone on the entire event.
➼ Participants should have any QR Code scanner application.

2. Connections

➼ 2 members per team.
➼ There are two rounds those who are getting high points in first round going to face second round.
➼ In First round, there will be 15 seconds for each question.
➼ The picture will be display on the screen , the participants should analyse the picture, Connect the picture and tell the answer.
➼ Second round is based on buzzer system. Buzzer application will be revealed on spot.
➼ Mobile Phones are allowed with high internet speed.

3.Mindsnap Data Puzzle

➼ 10 Teams are allowed.
➼ 2 members per team.
➼ Round 1: The images will be displayed on the screen for 20 seconds.
➼ Participants will have 20 seconds to observe and memorize the images on the screen.
➼ After 20 seconds, The participants there will be given 10 seconds to recall the things from displayed images.
➼ Points will be awarded for each correct recalled things.
➼ The first 7 team which has scored the highest points will be selected for round 2.
➼ Round 2: The images will be displayed on the screen for 10 seconds.
➼ Participants will have 10 seconds to observe and memorize the images on the screen.
➼ After 10 seconds, The participants are asked to recall the names of the things in the order of which images are shown.
➼ Points will be awarded for each correctly identified item.
➼ The selected teams will enter round 3.
➼ Round 3: A video will be played on the screen.
➼ Participants will be given designated time to carefully observe the video.
➼ After the video, questions related to the content of the video will be asked.
➼ The first team to buzz in or raise their hand gets the opportunity to answer.
